Citibike Data¶

Citibike is a bike sharing program in New York City. Each time a bike is checked out of a kiosk and returnd, data about the trip and the user is recorded and made public. This is a massive dataset that I thought would be fun to play around with a bit and see what pops out. I am using the pandas package to wrangle the data.

The first obvious insight we see in the data is a strong seasonal dependence, with ridership reaching a minimum during the winter. Interestingly, we can also see the initial increase in ridership when the program was first introduced in July of 2013.

Citibike offers both annual memberships as well as daily or weekly passes. In the dataset, annual membership holders are labeled as "Subscriber", while all other riders are labeled as "Customer". Interestingly, in spite of the fact that annual membership offers an increase in the ride time limit from 30 minutes to 45 minutes, subscribers tend to take significantly shorter rides.
