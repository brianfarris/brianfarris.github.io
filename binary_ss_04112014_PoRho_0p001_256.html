On the left we are looking at the surface brightness. Note in particular the brightness of the streams. Presumably this is due to shock heating. On the right we have the time-averaged radial profile of dL/dr. Note that at each annulus the brightness exceeds that of the shakura-sunyaev solution for a single BH. This is quite different from the picture people usually describe where they assume a gap in emission from the cavity.
